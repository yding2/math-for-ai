# Week 1: Why Mathematics for AI?

## Overview

This week introduces the role of mathematics in artificial intelligence and shows how mathematical models help us represent and solve real problems.

## Learning Objectives

By the end of this week, students will be able to:

- Explain why mathematics is important for AI.
- Identify variables, functions, and mathematical models.
- Use simple Python code to explore mathematical ideas.
