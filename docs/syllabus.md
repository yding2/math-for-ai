# Mathematics for AI

## Course Syllabus

**Instructor:** Yuhan Ding  
**Institution:** Illinois Institute of Technology  
**Term:** Spring 2027

## Course Description

This course introduces the mathematical foundations of artificial intelligence through intuitive explanations, computational experiments, and practical AI applications.

## Topics

- Python and data
- Vectors and geometry
- Matrices
- Functions and models
- Derivatives
- Optimization
- Probability
- Neural networks
- Large language models
